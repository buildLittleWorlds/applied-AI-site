The Sailor's Dream

Verse 1:
On the open sea, where the winds are free,
A sailor dreams of destiny.
The waves they crash, the sails they rise,
Underneath the endless skies.

Chorus:
Oh, the sailor's dream, a life of might,
With stars above to guide the night.
Through storm and calm, he'll always steer,
For freedom's call is ever near.

Verse 2:
Beneath the waves, the creatures play,
In the deep where mysteries lay.
With every tide, a tale unfolds,
Of ancient lands and treasures bold.

(Chorus)

Bridge:
The lantern's glow, a beacon bright,
On moonlit nights, a sailor's light.
With heart and soul, he'll navigate,
The unknown seas, his destined fate.

(Chorus)

Outro:
So here's to dreams that never die,
To endless waves and open sky.
The sailor's heart, forever free,
A timeless song upon the sea.
